About Us

Our Journey


We started Two Piece back in 2023 with a dream to make it easier for One Piece fans to buy affordable and high-quality products.


We took our products to Disneyland, where we put up a big sign asking people if they thought we should give up our jobs to make mugs and posters with One Piece characters on them. Underneath the sign, we had a QR code that took people to a website where they could vote 'yes' or 'no. At the end of the weekend, we tallied up the votes and it turned out that 100% of the votes were for 'yes', so we resigned from our jobs the next day and got cracking.


A few hours after being job-free, we found out that the 'no' button was, in fact, broken.


A month later, we had a fully functional website, enough products to fill a medium-sized swimming pool with, and zero tolerance for the bloke who created our initial voting poll.

To date, we've delivered more than 9000 One Piece products to fans. That's a lot of labels!

Our Very Important Mission


We believe that our products have great sentimental value for our members, so we want to enable fans across the globe to access our merchandise without breaking the bank.


In order to do this, we've put a scheme into action called "All For One. One for All." Every purchase made on our website will take 2% of its profit and collect it in a pot. Every month, the pot will reveal how much money was gathered and will be used to distribute Two Piece merchandise to our members across the globe - for free! Even if you've never made a purchase on our website, you'll still be in with a chance to get a free box of products delivered to your doorstep if you've created an account with us.


If you have any ideas of how we can make our website, processes or products better, feel free to contact us via email or post.
